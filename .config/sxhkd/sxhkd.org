* OPEN APPLICATIONS
#+begin_src txt :tangle ./sxhkdrc
super + ctrl + {d, e, a, f}
      { emacs --bg-daemon=local, emacsclient -c --socket-name=local, alacritty, firefox } &
#+end_src

** Restart Daemon
#+begin_src txt :tangle ./sxhkdrc
super + shift + r
      killtify sxhkd "Config reloaded" && sxhkd &
#+end_src

* OS FUNCTIONs
** Changevolume
#+begin_src txt :tangle ./sxhkdrc
super + ctrl + {Left, Right, m}
      changevolume { down, up, mute } &
#+end_src

** Change Keyboard Layout
#+begin_src txt :tangle ./sxhkdrc
super + alt + e
      changekblayout &
#+end_src
